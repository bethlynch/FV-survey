Healthy Eating Check-In

Quick survey — about 2 minutes

The next 7 questions ask about food you ate or drank during the past 7 days.


Think about all the meals and snacks you had from the time you got up until you went to bed. Be sure to include food you ate at home, at restaurants, or anywhere else.

Before we begin
Please enter a participant ID to continue.

7 questions · ~2 minutes

Question 1 of 7
Fruit 🍎
During the past 7 days, how many times did you eat fruit?
Do not count fruit juice. Include fresh, canned, or frozen fruit.
Vegetable 🥗
During the past 7 days, how many times did you eat green salad?
Include any green salad with lettuce, spinach, or other leafy greens.
Vegetable 🥔
During the past 7 days, how many times did you eat potatoes?
Do not count french fries, fried potatoes, or potato chips. Count baked, mashed, or boiled potatoes.
Vegetable 🥕
During the past 7 days, how many times did you eat carrots?
Include raw, cooked, or canned carrots, and carrot sticks.
Vegetable 🥦
During the past 7 days, how many times did you eat other vegetables?
Do not count green salad, potatoes, or carrots — you already counted those. Examples: corn, broccoli, green beans, tomatoes, peppers, peas, spinach cooked.
Drinks 🥤
During the past 7 days, how many times did you drink soda or pop?
Do not count diet soda or diet pop. Include regular Coke, Pepsi, Sprite, and similar drinks.
Breakfast 🍳
During the past 7 days, on how many days did you eat breakfast?
Count any morning meal, no matter how small.
