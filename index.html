Fruits & Vegetables Check-In

Quick survey — takes about 2 minutes

We're going to ask you 6 short questions about how often you ate or drank fruits and vegetables over the past 30 days.


Think about all forms — fresh, frozen, canned, cooked or raw — and include meals and snacks at home and away from home.

Before we begin
Please enter a participant ID to continue.

6 questions · ~2 minutes

Question 1 of 6
Fruit 🍊
How often did you drink 100% pure fruit juice?
Examples: orange juice, apple juice, grape juice. Do not include fruit drinks with added sugar (like Hi-C, lemonade, or Kool-Aid).
Fruit 🍎
How often did you eat fruit? (Not counting juice)
Include fresh, frozen, or canned fruit. Examples: apples, bananas, oranges, berries, watermelon, grapes, mango.
Vegetable 🫘
How often did you eat cooked or canned beans?
Include refried beans, black beans, kidney beans, lentils, chickpeas, edamame, tofu, bean soup, hummus. Do not include green beans.
Vegetable 🥦
How often did you eat dark green vegetables?
Examples: broccoli, spinach, kale, collard greens, romaine lettuce, chard, bok choy, arugula, mustard greens.
Vegetable 🥕
How often did you eat orange-colored vegetables?
Examples: carrots, sweet potatoes, pumpkin, winter squash (butternut, acorn, spaghetti squash).
Vegetable 🥗
How often did you eat other vegetables?
Not counting what you already told us about. Examples: tomatoes, corn, peas, lettuce, cabbage, peppers, baked or mashed potatoes, eggplant, cucumbers, onions. Do not include fried potatoes.
